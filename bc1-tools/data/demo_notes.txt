Because nobody wants to announce their sword-drawing intentions to an entire train car at 7:45 AM. Discretion is the better part of valor, and also of not getting reported to transit security.

We know what you're thinking: is a 500-year-old weapon technology really the answer to modern commuting stress? Kurogane Dynamics says — probably not, legally speaking, in most jurisdictions. But it sure looks incredible leaning against your desk.
